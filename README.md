# 대한건곤공사 (Daehan Geongon Public Corporation)

> A Korean mythology-based office drama, framed as a public sector 
> recruitment site.
> An extended IP set in the working world of supernatural beings — 
> where the Korean afterlife runs on civil service.

🌐 **Live Site**: https://myanadi.github.io/daehan-geongon/

---

## Overview

**대한건곤공사** is a worldbuilding IP that extends the universe of 
*계룡 은비 고등학교*. After graduating from Eunbi Highschool, supernatural 
beings enter the workforce — and many of them end up here, at the 
state-run Korean Heaven & Earth Public Corporation.

The site adopts the visual format of a real Korean public sector 
recruitment homepage, but reveals a workplace populated by deities, 
spirits, demons, and ghosts working office jobs.

## Concept

- **4 Divisions, 4 Mythological Domains**:
  - 명부계 (Underworld Affairs) — death and judgment
  - 신선계 (Celestial Affairs) — heaven and immortals
  - 토속계 (Folk Affairs) — local deities and household spirits
  - 명리계 (Order Affairs) — cosmic balance and fate
- **Public Sector Format**: Bureau structure, hierarchy, exam-based 
  hiring, and inter-department politics
- **IP Extension**: Eunbi Highschool graduates appear here as workers, 
  bridging two life stages of the same characters

## What This Site Does

- Recruitment-portal styled interface mimicking real Korean public 
  corporation sites
- Each division has its own page with mission, characters, and 
  application route
- Designed as a structural counterpart to Eunbi Highschool — 
  same IP, adult/professional life stage
